Piece By Piece

A single brick itself is of no great use but when several bricks are held together, one by one, it can be transformed into massive structures. The theme of our event revolves around the same. Just like the tiny puzzle blocks form a big picture, life is a puzzle too, made up of numerous puzzle blocks in different stages of our lives.
