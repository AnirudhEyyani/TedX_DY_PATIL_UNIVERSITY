TEDxDYPatilUniversity 2022

Our Journey So Far

TEDxDYPATILUNIVERSITY has an impressive legacy of collaborating with a myriad of universities to bring novel ideas from wholly different worlds into the limelight. We have been a consistent and steady abode to TED for the past two editions and the legacy is being carried forward this year as TEDxDYPATILUniversity 2022-23 comes to life. We strive to make our motto concise but one which encapsulates the essence of TED that is “Ideas Worth Spreading”.

Piece By Piece

Our university lives by unity, sync and synergy. DY Patil University is a learning ground for 16 schools sheltering students from all directions on the compass and all walks of life. But this was not one of those overnight miracles. Everyday, part by part, emotion by emotion, the great minds of this university have watered the seeds of merits and educated intentions on this field and every brick can vouch for the values these walls have accumulated within themselves.

Our world has been hurt because of the pandemic and we are, hopefully, in a rejuvenating cycle of recovery in terms of faith, humanity, civilisation, perspective and belongingness. We, at DY Patil University, are joyous to see our students re-enter the campus and blow life into its corridors. Slowly but steadily, things are going back to the new definition of normal and the fact that we are all coming together everyday is gratifying. Our theme “Piece by Piece” intends to incorporate the pieces of our hearts that have been beating in unison to bring this emotion alive.
