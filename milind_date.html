Mr. Milind Date

Flute Virtuoso

Milind Date is an esteemed musician,versatile composer, innovator and a traditionalist. He is famous for his exquisite Bansuri. Known by the names of 'Virtuoso' and 'Stage King', Mr. Date has accomplished many notable milestones in his career spanning over 35 years and counting, including composing 12 novel raags, delivering more than 3500 plus astonishing performances and collaborating with more than 150 of the world's finest musicians. His remarkable portrayal of the essence of Indian culture through his stellar music has earned him several award nominations, including the Emmy and GiMa.

Mr. Date has composed the score for the blockbuster movie "Among the Believers." His penchant for experimentation has allowed him to collaborate with other international musicians from various parts of the world. Several illustrious dignitaries and significant figures, such as Russian President Hon. Vladimir Putin and Indian President Hon. Bharat Ratna, Hon. Pranab Mukharjee have taken delight in his enchanting performances.

Everyone has music withing themselves
