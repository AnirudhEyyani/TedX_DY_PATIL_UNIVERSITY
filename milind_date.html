Mr. Milind Date

Flute Virtuoso

Milind Date is an esteemed musician,versatile composer, innovator and a traditionalist famous for his exquisite Bansuri. Known by the names of 'Virtuoso' and 'Stage King', Mr.Milind Date has accomplished many notable milestones in his career spanning over 35 years and counting, including composing 12 new raags, delivering more than 3500 plus astonishing performances and collaborating with more than 150 of the world's finest musicians. His remarkable portrayal of the essence of the Indian culture through his stellar music has earned him several award nominations including the EMMYs and GiMA.
