Mr. Puneeth Bekal

Marketing Director at Mastercard

Mr. Puneeth Bekal is an established marketing professional with a rich experience spanning 15 years. He is a connoisseur at managing large-scale campaigns for multiple titles in the industry, like CEAT, Lodha, and Godrej. He also handles digital activation, media innovations, and content strategy duties. He is currently making waves as Director of Marketing at Mastercard and has a proven track record in consistently meeting and surpassing targets in a range of market subsectors.

He has spearheaded business and brand transformation projects with AT Kearney, Bain Consulting, Landon & Interbrand. Mr. Bekal has a prolific career history, having held former positions like General Manager, Marketing at Godrej and Brand Manager at Lodha. He has successfully orchestrated 5 of India's most extensive award-winning campaigns to drive business and brand impact for Fortune 500 companies across FMCG, Consumer Durables, Retail, Real Estate & Fintech with noteworthy accolades like Emvies, Effies, Abbys, Smarties, Mobbys, Golden Mikes and more.
